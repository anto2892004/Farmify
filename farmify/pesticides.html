Cotton

Pesticides for cotton include insecticides for controlling pests like bollworms, herbicides for weed control, and fungicides to prevent fungal infections like cotton rust.

Coconut

Pesticides for coconut crops include insecticides to protect against coconut beetles, herbicides for weed management, and fungicides to control diseases like stem bleeding.

Sugarcane

Pesticides for sugarcane include insecticides for controlling pests like sugarcane borers, herbicides for managing weeds, and fungicides for diseases like red rot.
